##  Wedding Planning Checklist
*A structured timeline for executing luxury weddings.*

### Phase 1: Pre-Production (6-12 Months Out)
- [ ] Define the budget and guest count.
- [ ] Venue selection (Destination vs. Local).
- [ ] Finalize the wedding theme and mood board.
- [ ] Book key vendors (Makeup Artist, Photographer, Caterer).

### Phase 2: Design & DÃ©cor
- [ ] Mandap design and stage fabrication specs.
- [ ] Floral arrangement selection.
- [ ] Lighting layout (Ambient vs. Stage lighting).
- [ ] Entrance walkway concepts.

### Phase 3: Logistics & Execution
- [ ] Guest hospitality and transport management.
- [ ] Artist management (DJs, Live Bands, Performers).
- [ ] On-day coordination team briefing.

##  Corporate Event Logic
*Standard procedure for conferences, product launches, and meets.*

**1. Technical Setup**
* **Audio:** PA System, Lapel Mics, Podium Mic.
* **Visual:** LED Walls, Projectors, Clickers.
* **Lighting:** General wash for stage, spot for speakers.

**2. Branding**
* Standees, Backdrops, Registration Desk branding.
* Corporate gifting and kits.

**3. Flow of Event**
* Registration -> Welcome Address -> Main Presentation -> Q&A -> Networking/Dinner.

> **Resource:** Professional standards adapted from top [Event Organisers in Vadodara](https://www.thetuliptouch.in/event).
